## Overview

SoundMaster is an AI-Native all-in-one audio platform built for creators, developers and audio professionals.
Developed with native AI architecture, it covers the entire audio workflow from asset management, content creation to intelligent retrieval.

Core Features: 

- Audio Data Management: Standardized storage, classification and maintenance of audio assets
- AI Audio Generation: Generate voice, ambient sounds and various sound effects intelligently
- AI Music Generation: Create melodies, background music and original music automatically
- AI Sound Retrieval: Content-based audio similarity search and tag-based searching

A fully-featured audio toolkit to meet diverse demands of audio production and development.
